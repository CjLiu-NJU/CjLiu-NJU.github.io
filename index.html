Biography

Hi, I'm Chengjie Liu. I'm currently pursuing a Ph.D. at the Intelligence Sensing and Communication Lab (ISCL), Nanjing University (NJU), advised by Prof. Li Du and Prof. Yuan Du. I'm also doing an internship at National Center of Technology Innovation for EDA (NCTIEDA) advised by Prof. Jun Yang.

My research mainly focuses on knowledge - driven agile design of analog ICs. If you're interested in this field or want to engage in some interesting scientific research exploration together, please feel free to contact me by my Email:)
